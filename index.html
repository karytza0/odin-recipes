Description

spammos with rice and sea wed

Ingredients

  • spoms
  • sticky rice
  • swed

Steppos

  2. Spam slices in the pan (or raw, whatever)
  3. Get out the musubi shaper
  4. cut the swed into halves
  5. swed on the bottom, then rice, then spammo
  6. squish it with the squisher
  7. wrap the swed
  8. bam, spam
